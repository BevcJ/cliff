## Coverage Assessment

The source set is strong for visible employer/ATS postings, medium for Dutch-language public-sector discovery, and weak for hidden recruiter channels, LinkedIn-only jobs, Indeed-only jobs, and small-company hiring. Official CBS statistics are used to frame those gaps.
